#### <i class="fas fa-angle-double-right fa-lg"></i> **How to navigate**

To navigate to different GSAs and groundwater level decline scenarios, click the `GSA` and `Groundwater scenario` buttons in the left sidebar, scroll to the desired GSA and Groundwater scenario, and click to select.

Results shown correspond to the GSA, circled in <a style="color:red;">red</a>, and groundwater level decline circled in <a style="color:#b4dc8c;">green</a> as shown below.

#### <i class="fas fa-angle-double-right fa-lg"></i> **Controls**  

Controls are labeled in the image and described below: 

1. Groundwater level decline scenario, well failure count and percent, and cost estimate. Click to view methods.  
2. Map controls to click:  
  - zoom in/out  
  - full screen mode  
  - zoom back to home  
  - groundwater depth in feet below land surface (BLS) legend  
  - toggle between 2019 groundwater level and 2040 minimum threshold surface, and toggle GSA boundaries  
3. Hover for info. All forecasted well failures at various levels of groundwater level decline for the selected GSA are shown. The width of the bars shows the forcasted range of well failure. The red shaded area shows the selected groundwater level decline.

#### <i class="fas fa-angle-double-right fa-lg"></i> **Additional features**  

On the [main page showing all GSAs](https://www.gsawellfailure.com), click on a GSA in the map to view the forecasted well failure plot for the selected GSA, and hover over the plot to view failure and cost statistics for each scenario.
